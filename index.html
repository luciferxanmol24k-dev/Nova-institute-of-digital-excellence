FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for BCA and BBA?
Minimum 50% aggregate in Class 12 from any recognised board. Mathematics is preferred for BCA but not mandatory. For MBA, a bachelor's degree in any discipline with 50% marks plus CAT/MAT/NOVA-MAT score is required.
Are scholarships available?
Yes. Nova offers merit scholarships from 25% to 100% fee waiver based on academic performance, sports excellence, and financial need. Contact the admissions desk for a detailed scholarship brochure.
Does Nova offer hostel facilities?
Yes. Separate air-conditioned hostel blocks for male and female students with 24×7 security, Wi-Fi, laundry, gym access, and a recreation room. Apply early — seats fill fast.
What certifications do students receive?
Every graduate earns at least 4 industry certifications embedded in their curriculum at no extra cost: Google Analytics 4, Meta Blueprint, HubSpot Content Marketing, and one elective (AWS Cloud Practitioner, LinkedIn Marketing, or Semrush SEO).
How does the placement process work?
Career prep begins in Semester 3 — aptitude training, resume workshops, mock interviews, and GD sessions. Companies visit for on-site drives and a dedicated placement mentor is assigned to every final-year student.
Is the curriculum updated regularly?
Our Academic Advisory Board of 20+ industry leaders meets every semester. We guarantee no syllabus content will be more than 18 months old — essential in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
